> ### The Mission: Beyond chats and auto-complete
At Modus Zero, we believe the future of development isn't about "chatting" with an AI to help us code, but about **leading agents** that execute our technical vision.

Our flagship project, **[Mod0](https://github.com/ModusZero/mod0)**, embodies this shift:
* **Autonomic:** Plans, builds, manages, validates, learns, and iterates with minimal interaction and total user control.
* **Agnostic:** You choose your models, your keys, and your rules.
* **Agentic:** Leverages the true potential of the Agentic AI paradigm.

If you are tired of so many IDEs and are interested in the potential of "true vibe coding", check out our proposal.
